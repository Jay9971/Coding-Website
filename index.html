Power of Code

What do we do?

Power of Code is a club dedicated to promoting creativity and innovation, through app development, tutoring sessions, and so much more! In the emerging world of technological advancement, we strive to prepare and empower our club members for any job they find themselves committed to, and put their critical thinking, engineering, and scientific and mathematical skills to work.
For inquiries for volunteering and signing up for our club, check out the respective tabs on the top of our website!
