  • Restorative

    In partnership with the Restorative Justice Coalition

    What is the Restorative App?


    Restorative aims to provide an application which makes the process of offender rehabilitation more effective and efficient.


    Rehabilitation Process



    Development







    Offender Rehabilitation is oriented around educating offenders of their offenses and rehabilitating them.


    How will this work?


    • - education                                                        The Offender Rehabilitation process heavily involves the thorough education of offenders about their offenses. The goal is for offenders to understand exactly what their offense is, all of the effects of it, consequences of it, and why engaging in this offense was wrong. Depending on the offense, the education process will vary.




    - interactive + comprehensible interfaceThe main benefits that Restorative provides is that this education process will take place on an app specifically designed for it. Its well-structured, heavily interactive, and comprehensible interface is designed to engage offenders and allows for offenders to more easily absorb the often dense material provided to them.






    Development on this project will begin at the beginning of the 2022-2023 school year.


    Details:

    This app has a long road ahead of it, and achieving its full functional capacity will be quite the undertaking. This is why all the help we can get will be beneificial towards the completion of this project.

    What is Restorative Justice?

    Restorative Justice is an approach to justice that focuses on rehabilitation and healing as opposed to punishment like most modern justice systems (most notably the Retributive Justice System). This process works via a three party process between the victim, the offender, and the community. Click Here for more info on Restorative Justice!

    The Future

    The potential of this application expands beyond its interface's capabilities.
    Restorative has as a rehabilitation solution holds the potential to branch into the realm of A.I. This would give this application the ability to make intuitive observations about the offenders. These could include: studying the offender's knowledge base of the subject, their opinions on the subject, and how those opinions change over time.
