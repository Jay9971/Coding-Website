  • Restorative

    In partnership with the Restorative Justice Coalition

    What is the Restorative App?


    Restorative aims to provide a framework that makes the new Restorative Justice Program in SRVUSD more efficient and effective.


    This will be applied via two main sectors of the app:


    RESTORATIVE Desktop



    RESTORATIVE Webapp





    Restorative Desktop is oriented around educating offenders of their offenses and rehabilitating them.


    What will this add?


    • - interactive + comprehensive interface              The interface is designed in such a way that offenders using this application to become educated about their offenses is very easy to do. This is done by creating a well structured, visually appealing, and heavily interactive interface.




    - intuitive programmingThe intuitive programming aspect will allow for the app to assess the offender's understanding of the subject (being taught) at the beginning of the session and track that understanding throughout the session. The app will also assess the offender's opinions on the subject throughout the session as well, to determine if the user is growing or not. Eventually the app will determine potential root causes of the offenses and potential solutions.






    Restorative Webapp on the other hand is oriented around establishing a networked system for the entire RJ program.


    What will this add?


    • - a shared database of information       A shared database will be the main advantage presented by a networked application. All templates/text sets will be available across the network for human use and program use. Data collected from different sources of the network will be able to be collected and utilized by the intuitive programming on the Restorative Desktop to improve its own efficiency. This shared database will present massive ease of use for both people and programs.



    - a chat functionThe Restorative Webapp will offer a chat function that allows for offenders and counselors (who will be heavily involved in the process) to reach out to each other, as well as counselors and victims, and even offenders and victims if permitted by said victim.



    - a scheduling systemWith the massive complexities involved in this process, a scheduling system is a great benefit to the entire process. The scheduling system allows for counselors to schedule meetings with offenders or victims or whatever other meetings would be necessary in this process.


    What is Restorative Justice?

    Restorative Justice is an approach to justice that focuses on rehabilitation and healing as opposed to punishment like most modern justice systems (most notably the Retributive Justice System). This process works via a three party process between the victim, the offender, and the community. Click Here for more info on Restorative Justice!
